Privacy Policy for Classic Offline Games Room

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

Classic Offline Games Room ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the app, we may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as games played, high scores, and time spent in the app.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

1.2 Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app functionality and updates.
  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data (such as advertising IDs) to show personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase to understand app usage and improve the user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality.
  • Improve and personalize your experience.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends.
  • Show relevant advertisements.

3. Data Storage and Offline Support

Most of your game data (high scores, settings) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data remains on your device unless you clear the app's cache or uninstall it.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

5. Your Choices

You can manage your ad preferences through your device settings (e.g., "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Interest-Based Ads" on Android).

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
